ASTRIDE FINAL MARKET TEST BUILD 326 — HIERARCHICAL DRIVER APPROVAL

This package supersedes Build 325.

Final approval workflow
=======================
1. PROMOTER adds the Driver from Partner App.
2. Driver changes temporary password and uploads all five mandatory documents.
3. PROMOTER gives partial approval and sends the Driver to the Area Promoter.
4. AREA PROMOTER reviews and approves the Driver for Admin review.
5. ADMIN final-verifies each document and gives final approval.
6. ADMIN may bypass the Area Promoter stage only after Promoter approval,
   only after all documents are final-verified, and only with a written reason.

Authority rules
===============
- Promoter can add Drivers only under their own account.
- Area Promoter cannot add Drivers.
- Promoter cannot final-activate a Driver.
- Area Promoter cannot final-activate a Driver.
- Only Admin final approval makes a Driver eligible to go online.
- Admin cannot bypass Promoter partial approval.
- Admin can bypass Area Promoter approval with a recorded reason.

Partner App
===========
- Promoter-only Add Driver button.
- Temporary Driver login creation.
- Driver documents and approval chain visible.
- Promoter partial approval action.
- Area Promoter approval action.
- Role/scope checks enforced by backend.

Admin Console
=============
- Three-stage approval chain visible.
- Final document verification.
- Final Approve button after Area Promoter approval.
- Approve without Area Promoter button with mandatory reason.
- Reject and Suspend controls retained.

Deployment
==========
1. Deploy ASTRIDE_FINAL_HIERARCHY_BACKEND_ADMIN_v3.16.0.zip to site1
   with Overwrite, then recycle Application Pool.
2. Open Admin Console in Incognito or clear its cache.
3. Apply this full Build 326 source to GitHub.
4. Wait for Android Compile Check and Mobile Quality Gate.
5. Signed Release: App all, production, Version 3.16.0, Build 326.
