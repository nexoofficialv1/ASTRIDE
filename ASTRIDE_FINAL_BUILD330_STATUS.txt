ASTRIDE FINAL MARKET TEST BUILD 330 — LIVE DISPATCH & DRIVER CANCELLATION

What changed
============
Driver App
- Cancel assigned ride before trip start.
- Cancellation reason is mandatory.
- Cancellation is recorded against the Driver.
- Passenger booking returns to SEARCHING automatically.
- The cancelling Driver is excluded from the same booking request.
- Cancellation is blocked after OTP verification / trip start.

Passenger App
- While SEARCHING, shows the number of free eligible nearby Drivers.
- Displays privacy-rounded nearby vehicle markers on the map.
- Polls dispatch status every five seconds.
- Explains when no verified free Driver is available.
- Updates when Admin or a Driver assigns the ride.

Admin Console
- Live Rides -> Map & Assign.
- Shows passenger pickup, destination and eligible free Drivers on OSM.
- Displays distance, vehicle and rating.
- Admin can assign a selected Driver or auto-assign the nearest.
- Refreshes live dispatch information every five seconds.
- Shows Driver cancellation history and ride events.

Eligibility
===========
A nearby Driver appears only when:
- Final approved and not suspended
- Online with a current location
- Free from another active ride
- Inside the configured search radius
- Compatible with the requested ride type
- Has not cancelled this same booking
