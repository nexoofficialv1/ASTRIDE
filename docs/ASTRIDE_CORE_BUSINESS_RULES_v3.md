# ASTRIDE Core Business Rules v3.0

Implemented in Sprint 1:
- Full Toto, Share Toto and Motorcycle fare rules.
- Outside-area restriction: Full Toto only; maximum Toto route distance 29 km.
- Night restriction: Share Toto unavailable; Motorcycle requires eligible online driver.
- Passenger payment preference and driver payment-capability matching.
- Company commission cap and promoter/area-promoter split from company commission.
- Waiting charge calculation.
- Late-arrival compensation calculation.
- Promoter/Area Promoter hierarchy, scoped driver links, coaching audit and monthly-locked earnings data model.

The Admin Console UI and complete mobile screens remain later implementation work. All monetary and time values are designed to move to runtime configuration rather than requiring an app rebuild.
