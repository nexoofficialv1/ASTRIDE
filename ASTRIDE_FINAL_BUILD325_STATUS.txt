ASTRIDE FINAL MARKET TEST BUILD 325 — DRIVER VERIFICATION CONTROL

Build 324 is superseded by this package.

Authority
=========
Driver documents are verified by Admin only.
Promoters and Area Promoters do not receive approval authority.

Admin Console
=============
Drivers -> Review documents

Admin can:
- Open each uploaded document
- Approve or reject each document separately
- Record remarks / rejection reason
- Approve, reject or suspend the Driver
- See missing, pending, rejected and approved counts

Approval safety
===============
The Driver cannot be approved until all five mandatory documents are approved:
1. Identity document
2. Vehicle registration
3. Vehicle photo
4. Driver profile photo
5. Bank passbook / cancelled cheque

A rejected document immediately makes the Driver ineligible to go online.
The Driver may upload a corrected document and submit again.

==========
1. Deploy ASTRIDE_FINAL_ADMIN_VERIFICATION_BACKEND_v3.15.1.zip to site1
   with Overwrite, then recycle Application Pool.
2. Apply this full Build 325 source to GitHub.
